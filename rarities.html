Rarity Tiers

  • INSANE — The rarest tier, almost impossible to find. Only the luckiest players have these.
  • Mythical — Extremely rare and powerful, these rarities are highly coveted.
  • Legendary — Highly sought after with unique features or abilities.
  • Epic — Very rare and often stronger than common Jolers.
  • Rare — Uncommon, providing a good advantage in the game.
  • Uncommon — Found occasionally, better than the most basic.
  • Common — The most basic rarity, found everywhere and easy to obtain.
